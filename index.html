About Us

Anugrah Berkat Berniaga is a company that produces selected betel nuts grown and dried by Indonesian Jambi farmers. Its production reaches 200 tons per month.

We work closely with Jambi betel nut farmers where this betel nut has a higher tannin content and less water content than other superior betel nut and has a lot of white fiber in the dried betel nut. This betel nut has become a national superior va riety since the issuance of the Minister of Agriculture Decree Number 199/Kpts/ SR.120/1/2013. Then the right choice to choose the betel nut that we produce. The ripe orange yellow betel nut is dried by hanging for up to three weeks so that the betel nut is easily separated from the skin.

We produce these naturally dried betel nut in the factory with an oven and polish process, namely the betel nut is dried to death and cleaned so that the betel nut does not get moldy. So that the quality is maintained until it reaches the consumer. The export destination countries that become our consumers are India Thailand, China, Korea, India, Pakistan, UAE, Dubai and others.
